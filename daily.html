📿 3 Powerful Short Duas for Daily Life

1. Before Sleep:
“Bismika Allahumma amootu wa ahyaa.”
(In Your name, O Allah, I die and I live)

2. During Worry:
“Hasbunallahu wa ni‘mal wakeel.”
(Allah is sufficient for us, and He is the best disposer of affairs)

3. When Leaving the House:
“Bismillah, tawakkaltu ‘ala Allah.”
(In the name of Allah, I place my trust in Allah)

Let’s remind ourselves and others to make these du’as a part of our routine. They’re short, powerful, and carry great reward.
